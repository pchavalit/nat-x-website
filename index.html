01 — What we do

Four pillars, one discipline.

We operate across four distinct workstreams, unified by a single engineering culture: clean architecture, secure by design, and written to standards.

  1. 01

    Enterprise SaaS

    Subscription software for Thai enterprises and public-sector organisations, in verticals where the NAT Group has structural advantage — government, healthcare, transport, and industrial operations. Products are multi-tenant, secure by design, and built under the group's ISO/IEC 29110 software development lifecycle.

    Status Selected products in pilot; public launch dates to be announced.

  2. 02

    Specialty AI-Native Software

    AI software built for regulated industries, where quality, explainability, and compliance matter more than demo velocity. Typical engagements include:

    • AI-assisted document intelligence
    • Computer-vision systems for industrial inspection and operations
    • Thai-language speech-to-text and text-to-speech systems
    • Federated-learning platforms for privacy-preserving analytics
    • Domain-specific language-model applications for regulated industries
  3. 03

    Regulated Software Capability

    A software development capability aligned with the standards required for Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) — specifically, IEC 62304 lifecycle controls, with a roadmap to ISO 13485 quality management certification. Available to healthcare and life-sciences partners pursuing regulatory pathways including TFDA (Thailand), and on a roadmap basis, CE (EU) and FDA (US).

    Engagement Capability-based engagements under NDA, scoped to partner needs. We do not publicly discuss active SaMD programmes or clinical evidence work.

  4. 04

    Frontier Research

    Applied research in foundation models, federated learning, privacy-preserving machine learning, and domain-specific large language models for regulated industries. Research outputs feed product roadmaps and strengthen partner collaborations.
